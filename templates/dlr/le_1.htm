Difference
Low down-payment: a 24-month Red Carpet Lease means a lower down payment and less money up front than with a conventional loan.

More vehicle for the money: because leasing dollars go further than purchase dollars, a 24-month Red Carpet Lease means you can drive more vehicle for the same amount of money.

A new vehicle every two years: with a Ford Credit 24-month Red Carpet Lease, you can drive a new vehicle every two years for a payment that's competitive with a conventional four-year loan.

No up-front acquisition or lease end disposal fees: a Red Carpet Lease has no up-front acquisition or lease end disposal fees, something that can save you hundreds of dollars in additional charges.
